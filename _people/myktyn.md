Thomas Mykytyn is a graduate student studying geology at San Jose State University (SJSU). He is currently working with [SCI-CLAWPS]({% link _pages/home.md %}) investigating the distribution of mercury contamination in the Yolo Bypass, CA. While a student at SJSU, Thomas has worked for the Santa Clara Valley Water District, the US Geological Survey, and as a graduate teaching assistant for the [SJSU Geology Department](http://www.sjsu.edu/geology/). Thomas received his Bachelor of Science degree in geology from UC Davis in 2011. His primary advisor is [Manny Gabet](http://www.sjsu.edu/geology/people/faculty/gabet/index.html).
